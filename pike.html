A1.00 · PROGRAM + GOALS

The Pike Courtyard system is built around two constraints: a narrow rectangular yard and two existing palm trees. Every element — the risers, the tower, the stage — responds to those palms rather than fighting them. The goal was a space that could scale from a 50-person hang to a 1,000-person headline show without structural compromise.
